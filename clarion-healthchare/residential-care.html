Our Services

Residential Care Services

Safe, nurturing homes where young people receive the support they need to grow and thrive.

What We Provide

A home away from home

Our residential care centres provide warm, structured environments for young people who need short-term respite or long-term placements. Every service is designed around the individual — their needs, their story, and their potential.

We work in close partnership with Tusla and the HSE to ensure every young person in our care receives a consistent, high-quality experience that prioritises their safety, wellbeing, and personal development.

Our Model

Grounded in attachment theory

Our model of care is built on pro-social modelling and attachment theory — we believe that a young person's ability to form trusting, secure relationships is the foundation for everything else.

By creating environments of consistency, warmth, and clear boundaries, we help young people develop the emotional regulation, social skills, and self-belief they need to flourish long after their time with us.
